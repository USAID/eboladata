Ebola Data

Meeting notes from the Ebola Data Meetings between USG and non-USG Partners

View the Project on GitHub USAID/eboladata

The following are notes from the meetings held between key USG agency and non-USG stakeholders responding at various level to the Ebola outbreak in Western Africa. The purpose of these meetings are to introduce key actors and begin a dialogue about haves and needs for data, resources, tools and then take those conversations offline to the mailing list or elsewhere. The following participants included does not represent all of the individuals representing their respective agencies and organizations and the consortium hopes to hear from more using the established channels of communications.
